How it works

1
Pick a bounty Browse the board below. Click any bounty to open the GitHub issue and read the full spec.
2
Claim it Comment on the issue to request to work on it and share your Lightning address. If it's still available, a maintainer will label it In Progress. You must share your Lightning address before a maintainer can mark it in progress. Multiple hunters can work the same bounty — it may be assigned to you and others until a winner is chosen.
3
Build & submit a PR Work in your own fork. Join the public Discord server, post in #public-development, and get maintainer approval before opening a pull request. Once approved, link your PR to the issue. Your PR will go through three rounds of review before a decision is made.
4
Get paid in sats Once your PR is approved and merged, payment is sent directly to your Lightning address. No delays.

Bounty levels

⚡
Spark Good first issues. Small scope, clear requirements. Great entry point if you're new to the codebase.
🔥
Flame Mid-complexity features and fixes. Requires some familiarity with the Flash stack.
🌋
Eruption Hard problems. Significant engineering effort. Higher reward. Deep codebase knowledge required.
🏔️
Summit Legendary difficulty. Architecture-level work. The biggest rewards on the board. Not for the faint-hearted.
